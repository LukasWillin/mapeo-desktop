Welcome to Mapeo

Offline collaborative mapping

Mapeo is an offline map editor.

Mapeo makes it easy for individuals or teams to create maps and organize stories and knowledge.

Mapeo has been built in collaboration with indigenous communities in the Amazon.

The data you create is stored privately on your computer. You can share it with other Mapeo users via a sync file, or export it to GeoJSON.

This is a preview of Mapeo, it is in continued development and we are building an accompanying mobile app. Sign up on our website to receive notifications when new versions are released.

We welcome your feedback and reporting of bugs, please get in touch through our GitHub repository.
